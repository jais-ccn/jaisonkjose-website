About Me

"Every frame is a choice — between what to reveal and what to withhold. That tension is where the story lives."

I'm Jaison K Jose, a Boston-based cinematographer and photographer dedicated to crafting visually compelling stories through film and photography. My work blends technical precision with a genuine artistic instinct — creating visuals that feel both polished and deeply authentic.

My creative focus spans cinematic storytelling, behind-the-scenes documentation, live events, and branded content. I approach every project with a sharp eye for detail and emotion, whether I'm on the floor of a concert arena or on set for a commercial campaign. Notable work includes coverage of A.R. Rahman's Wonderment Tour — capturing the raw energy, artistry, and unforgettable moments of a global live music experience.

From high-profile concerts and commercial shoots to creative collaborations and documentary-style production, I'm dedicated to producing impactful visuals that connect with audiences and elevate every brand, artist, or story I work with.
